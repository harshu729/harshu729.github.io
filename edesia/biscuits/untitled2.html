Founded by Kaveri Goswami, Edesia delivers catering excellence through authenticity and culinary expertise. Kaveri started her journey as a gastronomical enthusiast by pursuing a baking course by the Head of Patisserie at Sophia’s College, Mumbai. Her skilled expertise as a home chef got her to feature on BBC Good Food Guide. In 2013, Owner and Proprietor Kaveri started her venture as a home chef and eventually started a commercial kitchen, to keep up with the growing demand. Today Edesia is known for it’s food standards and catering finesse. “Our philosophy has always been to use the freshest ingredients to bring out the finest flavours in every dish”, says Kaveri. The new entity of Edesia Caterers is Khane Khaas- Indian Zaika.
