Season's Speciality

Kaeng Phet: Thai curry

The ultimate comfort food, the authentic Thai Curry is the perfect blend of fresh ingredients with aromatic herbs and spices. Our signature Thai curry is made by grounding ingredients like ginger, garlic, lemongrass, chillies and other Thai condiments in a mortar-pestle to get the perfect base. It is then cooked to perfection with organic coconut milk to give the perfect burst of flavours. Fresh vegetables like broccoli, carrots et cetera, added to the curry makes it a wholesome and hearty favourite.

Served best with piping hot rice, this speciality gives your soul the warmth it needs during the lazy monsoon season. The signature Thai curry can also have additions such as prawns and chicken. The 2 variants available in this south-east asian delicacy are: the red curry featuring a base consisting of red chillies, and the green curry featuring a base made of green chillies.
